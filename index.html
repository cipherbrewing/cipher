CIPHER BREWING

When we decided to launch our first coffee, we knew it had to be something special. At its core, this release exists because we are passionate about coffee and wanted to share something we genuinely love drinking ourselves.

This lot quickly became a favourite of ours. We worked closely with Darshan (Ammikulavi Estate) throughout the process, refining the roast through multiple iterations and countless cuppings. We also shared samples with experienced coffee professionals to gather feedback and ensure we were on the right track.

Our goal was simple — preserve the complexity, character and vibrancy of the coffee while allowing its unique flavours to shine through. The result is a coffee we are incredibly proud to share as our first release.

— Cipher Brewing
